# Privacy Policy

**App:** c47 Calc
**Last updated:** 2026-04-25

## Summary

c47 does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. The app
runs entirely on your device.

## Data the app does not collect

c47 does not collect, store off-device, or transmit:

- Personally identifiable information (name, email, phone number, address)
- Account credentials or authentication tokens
- Location data (GPS, network, or IP-based)
- Contacts, calendar, photos, files, or other device content
- Device identifiers (advertising ID, IMEI, MAC address, install ID)
- Usage analytics, telemetry, crash reports, or diagnostic data
- Calculator inputs, results, or session history

The app declares no Android runtime permissions and no `INTERNET` permission,
so it cannot make network requests.

## Data stored on your device

Any application state (for example, calculator register values or display
state) is stored locally on your device by the Android operating system. It
is not transmitted anywhere.

If you have enabled Android Auto Backup, the operating system may include
this local app data in your Google account backup according to your device
and Google account settings. This is a feature of Android, not of c47, and is
governed by Google's privacy policy. You can disable backup for this app in
your device's system settings.

Uninstalling the app removes its local data from your device.

## Third-party services

c47 does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, crash
reporting, or backend services.

## Distribution

When the app is distributed through an app store (such as Google Play), that
store independently collects information related to your download and
installation under its own privacy policy. c47 itself does not receive that
information.

## Children's privacy

c47 does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13. The app
is suitable for general audiences.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be committed to the project
repository, and the "Last updated" date above will be revised.
